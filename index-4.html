FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Troco is an escrow service app designed to protect buyers and sellers in transactions. As a purchaser, Troco ensures your safety by holding the payment securely and only releasing it to the seller after you confirm receiving the promised goods or services in satisfactory condition.

Troco monitors the delivery process using the details you provide. This ensures that goods are traceable, and payments are only released after verification, offering you peace of mind and financial security throughout the transaction.

Troco guarantees sellers that payment will be made once the agreed-upon inspection period passes or the buyer accepts the goods or services. This ensures that sellers are compensated fairly and promptly after fulfilling their obligations.

Troco facilitates three-party transactions for brokers, enabling them to easily open, manage, and conclude trades. This makes it simpler for brokers to operate securely and efficiently while minimizing risks.

Troco uses a robust and reliable payment system to minimize the risks of dealing with unknown parties. Payments are verified and held securely until both parties meet the terms of the agreement, reducing the likelihood of disputes or fraud.
